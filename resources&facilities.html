Resources & Facilities

President Diosdado Macapagal National High School (PDMHS) offers a wide range of resources and facilities that support both academic and personal growth. The school is dedicated to providing an environment that fosters learning, development, and creativity for all students. Below are the key resources and facilities available at PDMHS:

Well-Equipped Classrooms & Laboratories

PDMHS boasts modern, spacious classrooms designed to provide a conducive learning environment. The school has specialized laboratories for subjects such as Science, Computer Science, and Technology, ensuring students have access to practical learning experiences. With updated equipment and a curriculum aligned to national standards, these facilities offer students the opportunity to engage in hands-on learning and experiments.

Hygienic Cafeteria

PDMHS understands the importance of a healthy and nutritious diet for students. The school has a hygienic cafeteria that serves well-balanced meals to students. It ensures that the food is safe, nutritious, and suitable for the needs of growing teenagers, offering a variety of options to cater to different preferences and dietary requirements.

Sports

To promote physical fitness and overall well-being, PDMHS offers a variety of sports activities for students. The Sports Department provides access to facilities such as basketball courts, volleyball courts, and a well-maintained track for athletic events. The school organizes regular sports events and competitions, encouraging students to explore their athletic abilities and participate in physical activities.

Library

The school library at PDMHS is a vital resource for students, offering a wide range of books, reference materials, and academic resources. In addition to textbooks, students have access to reference books, journals, and research materials that supplement classroom learning. The library is a quiet space where students can read, study, or conduct research to enhance their knowledge.

Computer Lab

The computer lab at PDMHS is equipped with modern computers and high-speed internet. It serves as an excellent resource for students to develop digital literacy and engage in technology-based learning. Whether for programming, research, or multimedia projects, the computer lab provides students with the tools they need to excel in the digital age.

Transportation

PDMHS provides reliable transportation services to facilitate easy access to the school. The transportation system helps ensure that students from nearby areas can travel safely and comfortably to and from school. The school provides vehicles that are regularly maintained for student convenience and safety.
